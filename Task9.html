An ecosystem is a geographic area where plants, animals, and other organisms, along with weather and landscapes, interact to form a bubble of life. These ecosystems consist of biotic factors (living components like plants and animals) and abiotic factors (nonliving elements like rocks, temperature, and humidity..Every factor within an ecosystem depends on each other, either directly or indirectly. For instance, a change in temperature affects the types of plants that can grow there, which in turn impacts animals relying on those plants for food and shelter
